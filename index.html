Short Bio

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Texas at Arlington. My Ph.D supervisor is Dr. Dajiang Zhu. I obtained the B.S. degree and the M.S. degree in Computer Science from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2015 and 2018, respectively. My research interests include the discovery of fundamental principles of brain structure and function and their relationship, brain disorder, and interaction between Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Human Intelligence (HI).
